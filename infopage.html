AniTaku is a website selling and promoting products related to a type of Japanese culture. AniTaku is a partnership company that teams up with big name anime studios to bring new products. AniTaku sells anime, DVDs, Manga, and Cosplay. We are located in Toronto, Ontario near Humber College. We started in 2018 and want to help provide a good site for selling anime related products. We hope our company is more successful and we will reach out to more studios to ensure our customers get the best exclusive anime products. AniTaku works directly with anime studios to get exclusive and licensed products.
